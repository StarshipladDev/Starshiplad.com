How Crpto Works
A good first place to see how crypto,blockchains and blocks work is this video:

Youtube Link

Essentially , a 'blockchain' is a combination of every transaction or change to a set of things that has ever happened. Everyone has access to the blockchain in its current form, so through crowdsourcing confirmation of data, all data and transactions should be valid.
This means 'ownership' of data, like how many bitcoins someone owns, is not an acctualy stored value, but rather the sum of all transactions or changes of data that persons 'identity' has been in, as stored by the blockchain.
A user's 'identity' is a combination of their username and password, which are hashed together (each letter/character is turned into a number, run through a algorithim and then the output number is turned back into a different character. ) This creates a 'public' identifier that everyone can see and interact with via transactions in the block chain.
How Sarshipbucks Work


Starshipbucks are my own take on crypto currency and a way to test the functionality of blockchains.

First, to test the hashing algorithims I have made, There are 2 sections (7 & 8) where you can encrypt and decrypt data.
Enter either a word o number you would like to encrypt (7) or decrypt (8) and press the button ot display your text.

Next, find your public ID by entering a Username and Password in the 'Transaction' section (5). Press 'Get Public Username')
and your public 20-character username will appear nex to it.

To make a transaction, post a 20-character username into the 'to' field, an ammount of starshipbucks to transfer in 'ammount',
and your username and password, and click 'Make Transaction' to create the new block detailing tha transaction. (5)

Now your transaction block, along with all previous ones, is stored in the page. To show the whole encryped blockchain after
a transaction, press 'Show Crypto' (2). This will pos the entire encryped blockchain to the Title page (1).

After than, you can keep pressing 'Unhash Crypto', each time it will decrypt the latest block in the blockchain.

By decyrpting all transactions, a known ledger of what ammoun each indentity has can be found.
